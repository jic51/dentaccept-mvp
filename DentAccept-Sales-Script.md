# DentAccept — Sales Script / Guion de Ventas

## ENGLISH VERSION

---

### PHASE 1: THE HOOK (15 seconds)
> Get their attention with a problem they feel every day.

**If calling/emailing:**
"Hi Dr. [Name], quick question — do you track how many treatment plans your patients accept versus how many walk out saying 'let me think about it'?"

**If in person:**
"Hey, can I ask you something? When a patient leaves without scheduling a crown or an implant — how often do they actually come back?"

*(Wait for their answer. Most will say "not often" or guess a number.)*

---

### PHASE 2: THE PROBLEM (30 seconds)
> Show them the data behind what they already feel.

"Here's what the data shows: **54% of treatment plans never get scheduled**. That's not because patients don't need the work — it's because they don't understand it.

The average practice with about $940K in annual revenue is leaving **$200,000 to $300,000 on the table** every year from treatments patients need but don't accept.

And here's the worst part: **43% of patients who leave without scheduling never come back**. Not next week, not next month. Never."

---

### PHASE 3: WHY IT HAPPENS (20 seconds)
> Name the three reasons they hear every day.

"Patients say no for three reasons:

1. **'I don't understand.'** — You explain a root canal verbally, but they can't picture it. The information doesn't stick.

2. **'It's too expensive.'** — They compare the cost to nothing, not to what happens if they wait.

3. **'Let me think about it.'** — And then the decision window closes at the door."

---

### PHASE 4: THE SOLUTION (30 seconds)
> Show, don't tell. If you have a tablet, open the app NOW.

"That's exactly what DentAccept solves. It's a visual presentation tool — works on any iPad, no installation.

Your staff selects the procedure on the tablet. The patient watches a 20-second animation showing exactly what's happening in their mouth. Then they see what happens if they wait — the real timeline and the real cost of delaying.

All in English or Spanish with one tap."

*(If you have the app open, hand them the tablet and let them tap through a Crown or Implant procedure. Let the product sell itself.)*

---

### PHASE 5: THE PROOF (15 seconds)
> Data kills objections.

"Practices that use visual chairside tools see **78% case acceptance** compared to the 46% industry average.

And 64% of patients say they prefer digital animations over traditional brochures or verbal explanations.

This isn't my opinion — this is peer-reviewed data."

---

### PHASE 6: WHAT MAKES US DIFFERENT (20 seconds)
> Hit the 4 differentiators fast.

"Four things make DentAccept different:

1. **It's bilingual** — English and Spanish, one tap. 60 million Spanish speakers in the US. Most tools ignore them.

2. **Zero IT friction** — No installation, no downloads, no training. Open a browser on your iPad and you're running.

3. **The 'What If You Wait' module** — Most tools show the procedure. We also show what happens if they DON'T do it. That's what converts fear into action.

4. **No patient data stored** — Zero PHI. No HIPAA headaches. We only track aggregate analytics — how many presentations, acceptance rates."

---

### PHASE 7: THE CLOSE (15 seconds)
> Make saying YES the easiest option.

"Here's what I'd suggest: **try it for 14 days, completely free.** No credit card upfront. No contracts. No risk.

Put it on one iPad in one operatory. If even 2 or 3 extra patients per month schedule that crown or implant they were going to skip — that's $3,600 a month in recovered revenue on a $129 investment.

That's a 2,700% ROI. But honestly, the best way to see it is to just try it."

---

### HANDLING OBJECTIONS

**"We already use [Chairside/Modento/other]"**
> "Those are great tools. The difference is price and simplicity — we're $79/month vs $300-500, there's nothing to install, and we're the only one with built-in 'consequences of waiting' that leverages loss aversion. Plus full bilingual support. Would you try it side by side for 14 days?"

**"My patients don't need animations"**
> "I hear you. But the data says 64% of patients prefer visual explanations, and practices using them see 78% acceptance vs 46% without. Would it hurt to test it for 14 days and see what happens?"

**"I don't have time for another tool"**
> "That's exactly why we built it the way we did — zero setup, zero training. Your assistant opens it on the iPad, taps the procedure, hands it to the patient. The whole thing takes 20 seconds."

**"$79/month is still money"**
> "Totally fair. But if it helps just ONE extra patient accept a $1,200 crown per month — that's a 1,400% return. And you get 14 days free to prove it works before you pay anything."

**"I need to think about it"**
> *(Smile)* "That's exactly what your patients say, right? Here's the difference — this one is actually free to try. No risk, no commitment. Should I set you up with a code right now?"

"Oiga, le puedo hacer una pregunta? Cuando un paciente se va sin agendar una corona o un implante — que tan seguido regresan?"

Todo en ingles o espanol con un solo toque."
