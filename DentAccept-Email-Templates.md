## TIPS FOR SENDING

1. **Send 5-10 emails per day** — not more. Quality over quantity.
2. **Personalize the first line** — mention their city, practice name, or something from their website.
3. **Send Tuesday-Thursday, 7-9 AM** local time — highest open rates for professionals.
4. **Never send attachments** in cold emails — they trigger spam filters.
5. **Track who opens** — use a free tool like Mailtrack for Gmail.
6. **Follow up on opens** — if they opened but didn't reply, send Email 2 sooner.
7. **Don't use "free" in subject lines** — spam trigger. Use "try" or "test" instead.
8. **Find emails at:** practice websites, Google Maps listings, Healthgrades, ZocDoc, Yelp.
